# MoodTunes

A viral web app that acts as your personal 'music mood ring'. Users answer 3 quick daily questions about their mood, and the app generates a unique musical melody that represents their emotional state. Users can share their daily 'mood tune' on social media, compare with friends, and track their mood patterns over time through music.

Key Features:
- Interactive mood questionnaire with smooth animations
- Generative music based on mood inputs
- Social sharing
- Daily streak tracking
- Monthly mood patterns visualization

Tech Stack:
- HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
- Tone.js for audio synthesis
- Local storage for progress tracking
